# TASK-7-Identify-and-Remove-Suspicious-Browser-Extensions
Mini Guide: Managing Browser Extensions Safely

1. Open Extensions Manager

   In Chrome: Menu (⋮) > Extensions > Manage Extensions

   In Firefox: Menu (☰) > Add-ons and Themes > Extensions



2. Review Installed Extensions

   Look through all the listed extensions carefully.



3. Check Permissions & Reviews

   Click Details to see what data each extension accesses.

   Search online for user reviews or known issues.



4. Spot Suspicious or Unused Extensions

   Watch for unfamiliar names or extensions you don't remember installing.



5. Remove Unnecessary/Suspicious Extensions

   Click Remove or Disable for any extensions you don't trust or need.



6. Restart Your Browser

   Close and reopen your browser to apply changes and check for performance improvements.



7. Understand the Risks

   Malicious extensions can:

   Steal passwords or browsing data

   Inject ads or redirect searches

   Slow down your browser/system




8. Document Your Actions

   Note which extensions were removed and why.

   Keep a log for future reference or troubleshooting.
